# Solar Body Texture Sources

These textures are bundled for the public 3D solar system demo. They are used for educational visualization and are not intended for scientific analysis.

All final app assets are local WebP files in `public/textures/sun/`, `public/textures/planets/`, and `public/textures/moons/`. Source images were kept at their original available resolution when under the 2048px target width.

## Sun Texture Source

| Body | Local file | Source | Credit | Source size | Conversion |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sun | `sun/sun.webp` | https://science.nasa.gov/learn/heat/resource/sun-3d-model/ | NASA Science | 2048x1536 JPG inside USDZ | Extracted cube-cross texture, converted to 2048x1024 equirectangular, re-encoded to WebP q84 |
