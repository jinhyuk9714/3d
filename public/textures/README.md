# Planet Texture Sources

These textures are bundled for the public 3D solar system demo. They are used for educational visualization and are not intended for scientific analysis.

All final app assets are local WebP files in `public/textures/planets/`. Source images were kept at their original available resolution when under the 2048px target width.

| Planet | Local file | Source | Credit | Source size | Conversion |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mercury | `planets/mercury.webp` | https://space.jpl.nasa.gov/tmaps/mercury.html | Caltech/JPL/USGS | 1440x720 JPG | Re-encoded to WebP q84 |
| Venus | `planets/venus.webp` | https://science.nasa.gov/3d-resources/venus/ | JPL/Caltech | 1440x720 WebP | Downloaded NASA preview WebP |
| Earth | `planets/earth.webp` | https://science.nasa.gov/3d-resources/earth-a/ | USGS & NASA/JPL | 1280x720 WebP | Downloaded NASA preview WebP |

| Jupiter | `planets/jupiter.webp` | https://science.nasa.gov/3d-resources/jupiter/ | JPL & Caltech | 720x360 WebP | Downloaded NASA preview WebP |
